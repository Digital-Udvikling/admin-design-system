## Built for admin tooling

Tuned for the screens an operator sits in all day — dashboards, CRMs, ops consoles, settings panels. It favors information density over hero spacing, predictable primitives over decorative flair, and operator UX over marketing polish.

**Use this if** you're building a form with twelve inputs, a table with forty columns, or a control panel with a dozen actions.

**Look elsewhere if** you need a marketing site, a consumer-facing product surface, or anything where the goal is to *feel* polished rather than *operate* quickly.
